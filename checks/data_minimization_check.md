# Data minimization check

## Goal

Collect only what is necessary to answer the stated research or user support question.

## Evidence to look for

1. Each field maps to a stated purpose.
2. Optional fields are labeled optional.
3. Sensitive free text collection is avoided unless necessary.

## Pass criteria

No fields are collected without a clear purpose mapping.

## Fail examples

1. Collecting full legal name when not required.
2. Collecting detailed medical history without clear need.
